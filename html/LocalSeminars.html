Typical weeks have two seminars: a HEP-ML seminar at 2:30 Pacific hybrid at LBNL on Tuesdays and the BIDMaP seminar at noon Pacific at UC Berkeley on Thursdays. The Tuesday seminar is mostly focused on local particle and nuclear physics methods and applications while the Thursday seminar is roughly half fundamental physics (broadly defined) and half materials science. Please subscribe to hep-ml@lbl.gov to receive reminders and updates about meetings. Sascha Diefenbacher (SDiefenbacher@lbl.gov) organizes the Tuesday seminar and Dennis Noll (DNoll@lbl.gov) is the LBNL co-organizer for the Thursday seminar.
